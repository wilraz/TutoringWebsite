GeauxTutors - Policies


Our tutoring Service Policies:


  1. The regular hourly rate is $20/hr.

  2. One week before and during midterm exams and final exams, the tutor reserves the right to increase the hourly rate of all tutoring sessions to $25/hr.

  3. All scheduled sessions will be held in the LSU Middleton Library. However, when the library is closed, the tutor can still meet you at the Baton Rouge Main Library (7711 Goodwood Blvd, LA 70806) if you still want to meet on that day.

  4. Each session must be at least 1-hour long. If you, the client, decide to leave early, you will still have to pay for the whole hour. For example, you scheduled a 2-hour session, but you decide to leave after 1 hour and 30 minutes, you will still have to pay for the full 2 hours.

  5. If you, the client, want to cancel a scheduled tutoring session, you must notify the tutor at least 2 hours before the session starts, unless it is an emergency. Otherwise, the tutor reserves the right not to continue tutoring you anymore.

  6. You, the client, should try to be fully committed to your tutoring sessions. Frequent cancellations are not fair to the tutor who has arranged their schedules for you. If you cancel 2 scheduled sessions in a month, you will have to pay a $5 cancellation fee to the tutor in your next session with him/her. If you refuse to pay the $5 cancellation fee, the tutor reserves the right to not continue tutoring you.

  7. You, the client, will pay the tutor in cash at the end of each tutoring session.
